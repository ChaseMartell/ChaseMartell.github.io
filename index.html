About Me

My name is Chase Martell. I started coding during my freshman year of college. It wasn't something that I initially thought I would enjoy, but after I achieved my associates degree I decided to pursue Software Development. It all came from my love of computer games growing up, and interests in how computers are built. My main languages that I am most experienced with are Python and C++, which I have used for about 4 years now. In core classes I have developed proficiency in other languages such as Javascript, Java, and C.

I have recently branched into more front-end libraries and frameworks,doing projects with more advanced javascript and with React.js and Node.js. I recently worked an internship where I was able to apply my Python knowledge to work on voice recognition for an application. With this application, I was able to learn React Native as well! In my free time, I have been coding a video game with Unity while learning some C# and really great software design principles.
